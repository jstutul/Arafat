Overview

Expora is a comprehensive property management software designed to simplify the way Bproperty handles its diverse range of property services. It serves as a unified platform for managing everything from property listings to client interactions. With Expora, Bproperty's 500+ employees can streamline workflows, collaborate more effectively, and provide clients with a seamless experience in buying, selling, and renting properties. This user-friendly solution revolutionizes property management, setting new standards for efficiency in the prop-tech industry.

Problem

Bproperty faced challenges managing its diverse property services. Using multiple tools led to confusion and inefficiency among its 500 employees. They needed a unified solution to streamline workflows and provide clients with better service. Expora was born to solve these problems, making property management simpler and more effective for Bproperty and its clients.

Solution

Expora bring them all together in one place. Expora is the comprehensive solution designed to unify Bproperty's property management processes. With intuitive interfaces, seamless integration, and powerful features, Expora streamlines workflows, enhances team collaboration, and delivers exceptional client experiences, setting new standards in prop-tech efficiency.

Design

Design Ideation

Design ideation is a collaborative process of generating creative ideas and solutions to address specific design challenges or opportunities. It involves brainstorming, sketching, and exploring various concepts to inspire innovation and problem-solving. Through ideation sessions, teams can generate a wide range of ideas and concepts, fostering creativity and driving the design process forward.

Sketches & Wireframes

Sketches and wireframes are foundational tools used in the early stages of the design process. Sketches are quick, hand-drawn representations of design concepts, while wireframes are digital or paper-based outlines that define the structure and layout of a product or interface.

Brand Guidelines

Brand guidelines are a set of rules and standards that define how a brand should be represented visually across all touchpoints. They include specifications for elements such as logos, colors, typography, imagery, and tone of voice. Brand guidelines ensure consistency and coherence in brand communication, helping to build brand recognition and trust among audiences.

High Fidelity Design

High fidelity design refers to the detailed, polished version of a user interface or product design. It incorporates precise visual elements, such as colors, typography, imagery, and interactive components, to closely resemble the final product. High fidelity designs are used for testing and validation purposes, providing a realistic representation of the user experience before development.

Features and Sections

AUTHENTICATION

Deal Summary

All deals in one place. The deal will separated by category/pipeline such as SPA(sales and purchase agreement) Pipeline, Registration Pipeline. Users able to create task on the particular deal and view all details of a deal. Also show the Won/Lost deals.

Dashboard

The Expora dashboard is where it all begins. Here, users get a quick overview of all the features available. They can see deals across different pipelines, track task logs, monitor task completion ratios, view cash collection metrics, including service charges and total cash collected.

Leadsection

Users can easily search for leads using the client's phone number. They can also add new leads, which include comprehensive client information such as lead type (Buyer/ Seller/ Rental/ Interior/ Legal/ Mortgage), location details, lead specifics, amenities, and more.

Property Valuation

The property valuation provide a comprehensive overview of key factors essential for accurate property assessment. Users can input apartment or building features, legal status, and unit details. The platform offers cost evaluation tools and allows users to upload property photos for visual reference. Users can also access forecasting and comparative data on similar properties sold or rented in the neighborhood, empowering informed decision-making.

Property Valuation App

Bproperty's field employees require a mobile app to efficiently gather property data from various locations. This app seamlessly integrates with the Expora SaaS platform. Within the app, users can easily add new projects, accessing a range of options including project details, features, documentation, units, and pricing. The app also facilitates capturing property images. Once all information is inputted, users can submit it for review. Upon review by the Expora SaaS, the data can be approved or rejected, completing the process efficiently.
